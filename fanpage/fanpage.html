Step Out!

Stray Kids is an 8 member South Korean Band formed through the survival show of the same name under JYP Entertainment.

The name "Stray Kids" originally referred to a lost child who wants to chase their dreams and later evolved to represent the idea of finding a way together out of the ordinary

Stray Kids actively engages in the creative process, contributing to the composition and production of their music. This hands-on approach reflects their unwavering dedication to delivering an unparalleled and genuine musical experience to their global audience.

Beyond the melodies, Stray Kids serves as a lyrical voice for a generation, fearlessly addressing societal issues and personal narratives. Their commitment to crafting meaningful and relatable content establishes them as not just performers but as socially conscious artists.

Immerse yourself in their discography, and witness the seamless fusion of genres that define the Stray Kids sound. From the pulsating rhythms of hip-hop to the soul-stirring melodies of rock, each note is a testament to the group's dedication to pushing artistic boundaries.
